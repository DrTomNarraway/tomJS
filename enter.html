Objective of this Research Project

In this study, we want to determine how our experimental manipulation affects the speed and accuracy of your responses.

Study Procedure

First, you will use an ID-1 sized card to set the size of the stimuli on your screen. Then we ask for your age, gender, and dominant hand, but these details are optional. You will be asked to do decide if a simple visual stimulus is leaning to the left or right. For each decision we record how long you take to respond and if your response is correct or not. Sometimes you will be asked to respond when you are ready, and sometimes you will need to respond as soon as a bar at the top of the screen turns blue. The experiment takes approximately 60 minutes and will force your browser into full screen mode.

Reimbursement

You will be reimbursed 10 GBP on the condition that you meet your obligations.

Obligations

You are obliged to pay attention. In order to determine if this obligation is met, this study contains attention checks. As per Prolific policy, if you fail too many of these checks you must return your submission. Failing too many attention checks renders you entirley ineligible for payment.

You are obliged to complete the study. If you do not complete the study, regardless of the reason, you may still be eligible for partial payment (sometimes called pro rata payment) for your time. Each case must be individually assessed, but in general if you have provided valid responses to the trials that you did attempt, and did not fail your attention checks, we would consider you to be eligible for partial payment.

If you wish to withdraw consent to the use of your data, you are obligated to contact Tom Narraway before your submission is approved. After this time, your data is further anonymized and we therefore cannot identify your data to delete it.

Responsible Bodies

Data generated by this study is only processed by our research group and therefore the responsible body is the University of Bremen.

Categories of Data

As a part of this study, we collect only anonymous data.

Purpose of Data Collection

We collect your anonymous data for research purposes.

Legal Basis for Data Processing

The legal basis for the processing of your data is your consent according to Art. 6.1(a) GDPR.

When is your Data Deleted or Anonymized?

If your submission is returned or rejected, your data is immediately deleted. If your submission is approved, your data is immediately anonymized and kept indefinitely.

Who has Access to your Data?

Initially your data are only accessed by our research group, are not passed to any other parties and are not transferred outside of the EU/ EEA. After removing your prolific ID, your data may be made publicly available on scientific data repositories.

Protection from Unauthorized Access

Your data are initially linked to your anonymous Prolific ID. When your submission is approved: you are assigned a new random ID. This ensures that once your data is submitted: it is impossible for anyone, including us, to link your data back to you.

Your Rights

Because your Prolific ID is removed from the data once it is approved: you only have the right to revoke consent to the use of your data up until your submission is approved. After that time, we can no longer identify which data is yours and therefore cannot delete it. You may withdraw from the study at any time, without giving reasons, and if are otherwise eligible, may receive pro rata compensation for your time.
